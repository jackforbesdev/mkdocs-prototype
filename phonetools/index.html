    PhoneTools

    (AI generated MD for prototyping purposes)

    Welcome to PhoneTools documentation. PhoneTools is a quick-to-deploy TPS and CTPS screening solution for Salesforce that helps businesses stay compliant with UK data privacy laws when making cold calls.

    Overview

    PhoneTools screens your Salesforce records against the UK Telephone Preference Service (TPS) and Corporate TPS (CTPS) lists sourced directly from the DMA with daily refreshes. Stay compliant with ICO requirements and mitigate the risk of fines.

    Key Features

    • TPS and CTPS Screening: Screen phone numbers against both individual and corporate preference lists
    • Simple Traffic Light System: Instantly view screening status with clear visual indicators
    • Manual and Automated Screening: Use the Screen Phone Numbers button or set up automated batch screenings
    • Compliance Tracking: 28-day screening cycle tracking to remain compliant with ICO requirements
    • Salesforce Native: No need to leave Salesforce to check compliance status
    • Flow Integration: Queue immediate screening jobs to get results in seconds
    • Reporting & Workflows: Use screening status in reports, queues, and custom workflows
    • JSON Output: Leverage configurable Phone Screening Results field for custom development
